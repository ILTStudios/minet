About Us
What
"When I'm Gone" is a Rehabilitation Centre that revolves around helping our patients get back into their lives and stray away from an unhealthy life in the metaverse. Through our app or website you can communicate with us regarding your issues and experience with the metaverse. We can help you learn to live again in normal society and not depend on the metaverse for everything.
Where
We are situated throughout the world in over 20 different locations. We are located in all continents and in many countries. You can read more about our locations through our app. If by chance you can not reach us at the moment, we do provide on demand sessions with professionals, but we highly recommend you spend the time to fly out to us and spend the recommended time for your recovery.
Why
Ever since the metaverse went on the global market and became available throughout the globe, we have seen a lot of complaints and news regarding the mental health of its users. Which is why we provide those who have come back from the metaverse, rehabilitation back into our world. We provide the luxury of having the security to feel like you, even outside the digital world.
Our Services
Online/Offline
"When I'm Gone" provides a great user experience both in online and offline modes. We respect how our users would like to approach their rehab, and we understand if flying out to one of our centres is difficult.
Professionals
Through our Web App or through our mobile app, you can meet Professionals and people going through the same efforts as you are. We here at "When I'm Gone" strive to connect people together to help make friends and relationships that can help our customers.
Regular Updates
Keep yourself updated with the current news, and about all the new services we provide. We also update you and your friends about activities near your locality so that you can get out there and truly live in this non digital world.
Personal Analytics
From statistics about your sleep cycle, to calorie counters and health managers. We try our best to help you be up to track with your own health and self. These are just a few examples of all the great free services we provide our customers.
Accomodations and Activities
Our Rehabilitation Centres provides to its customers and patients the idea of a personal space where they can have the safety of others just like them as well as great Accomodation regarding rooms, food, activities. Our Professional therapists also live near the patients so that help is always near. We also have in our campus many organised events aimed at building new relationships between our customers.
Get Connected
01.
Contact Us.
To get started on your journey of healing, we recommend you take our test so that we can build up a personalised profile just for you. After that we will attempt to contact you, either physically or digitally depending on your prefence, with the help of the app. Take a free councelling session with a professional and see where you stand.
02.
Visit the Centre.
After we contact you, we do recommend you fly to one of our rehab centres, and spend the desired amount of time by your professional. At rehab we will provide you with the tools and resources necessary to rebuild yourself and your life. The centres itself are quite lovely and allow each patient to customise not only their living quarters but also let them socialise with others, partake in activities, and perform fun sessions.
03.
Use Our Services.
At rehab we recommend each of our patients to think of it as a vacation where they can reignite themselves in a new way. Where you can find yourself again, even after having lost it. After all, healing truly starts after you discover who you are.
04.
Staying Consistent.
After your recommended stay, we will have to send you back or keep you for longer based on your report. Even after rehab, we stay connected with our patients through the app where we assign each patient a regular check up and therapy session to see progress and steadiness. We care for each of our patients.
